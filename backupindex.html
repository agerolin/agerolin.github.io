I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics of the University of Jyväskylä. My research is funded by the Academy of Finland, project "Local and global structure of metric measure spaces with Ricci curvature lower bounds"  (PI: Tapio Rajala).

I did my Ph.D. degree at Università di Pisa under the supervision of Prof. Giuseppe Buttazzo.

Recent Activities & News:

- In Spring 2019, I will join Paola Gori-Giorgi's group at the Department of Theoretical Chemistry of the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. My research will be founded by a Marie Skłodowska-Curie fellowship (see Press Release)


- I am teaching this semester the course Topics in Mathematics of Data Sciences.

- I taught a Ph.D. course on Density Functional Theory and Optimal Transport.

- I am co-organizing the Geometry Seminar and administrating the website of the ERC GEOMEG project.

- I have co-organized the Workshop Optimal Transport meets Density Functional Theory (2017).
