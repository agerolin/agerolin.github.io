Our main activities

During the year, we take part in the following events:

Jyväskylä Researchers' night / Tutkijoiden yö

Generally, organized in September, together with the City of Lights in Jyväskylä (Valon kaupunki) and similar events all around Europe.

SoMath at your School / Kouluille


We develop many activities all over the year for upper secondary school students. Click here for more information and contact us.

JYU Open Days / Hakijan päivä


Every year in November at the main Campus of the University.


    Where to find us?

    The items of the exhibition are permanently at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics (3rd floor) and the Nanoscience Center (YN121) at the University of Jyväskylä.
