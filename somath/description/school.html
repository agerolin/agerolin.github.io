SoMath for Schools

One of the main goals of Signs of Mathematics is to build bridges between upper secondary school and research level mathematics.

Through our exhibition and website, we propose a transversal path on learning mathematical concepts and an interdisciplinary view of mathematics.

All our program can be developed at different levels, from kids at age of 12 years old to university students, their parents, and adults interested in thinking about science and mathematics.

We welcome teachers and educators to contact us in order to discuss possibilities of having SoMath at your classroom as well as to visit our exhibition.
