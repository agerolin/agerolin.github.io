Researchers' Night 2018

Program:  
  English  
  Suomi  



The University of Jyväskylä will once again participate in the European Researchers’ Night, simultaneously with 11 other locations in Finland and many other cities all over Europe, on the 28th of September. The full-day event presents science and researchers in different kinds of workshops, panel discussions, and exhibitions.

During the Jyväskylä Researcher Night, the Signs of Mathematics will be at the Department of Physics, Room FYS2 (YFL) from 4 PM to 22 PM.

More about RN 2018 at the JYU Website and follow the event on Facebook (only in Finnish)

All events are free.

Do you know that...

    The European Researchers' Night takes place yearly, typically starting on the last Friday of the month of September, and is the occasion for a Europe-wide public and media event for the promotion of research careers.

    The aims of that event is to bring researchers closer to the general public and to increase awareness of research and innovation activities, with a view to supporting the public recognition of researchers, creating an understanding of the impact of researchers' work on citizen’s daily life, and encouraging young people to embark on research careers.
